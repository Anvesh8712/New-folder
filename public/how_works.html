How It Works

  1. The writer submits their script along with details like genre, logline, etc.
  2. Your script is automatically forwarded to professionals with expertise in various genres for grading.
  3. 5-10 reviewers analyze your script and provide a rating.
  4. Ratings are based on various criteria like new concepts, screenplay originality, drama and humor quotients, and the climax.
  5. Receive feedback from experts to polish your script further.
  6. Scripts with ratings above 8 are shared with production houses. Writers get opportunities to meet decision-makers.
  7. Reviewers can participate in script discussions if the writer seeks further improvements.
